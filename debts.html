Debt rules
  • Debt rows are not money truth by themselves.
  • If money moved now, the backend must create the debt and origin ledger row together.
  • Debt-only rows are valid when account movement already exists or should not be duplicated.
  • Repayments are separate money movements and must use the canonical payment flow.
  • Missing ledger repair must be explicit; no legacy debt is auto-repaired blindly.
