Salary contract rules
  • The most recently saved contract is the active forecast source.
  • Payday is the day of month the salary is expected to land.
  • Net salary is what Forecast uses for expected income.
  • Deductions are stored for reference but Forecast reads net directly.
  • Only one contract per effective month is kept.
